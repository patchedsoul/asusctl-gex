asusctl-gex is a frontend for some functionalities of asusctl and supergfxctl that were born inside the asus-linux.org community.

asusctl is not required
supergfxctl is required if you have a dual GPU laptop and want to switch between various GPU modes. It is tested on a variaty of laptops including Intel / Nvidia, AMD / Nvidia, Intel / AMD and AMD / AMD GPU combinations.

To learn more about it, please have a look at:
https://gitlab.com/asus-linux/asusctl
https://gitlab.com/asus-linux/supergfxctl
https://gitlab.com/asus-linux/asusctl-gex

https://asus-linux.org/
